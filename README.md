## The 5 Feedback Items

### #1 — GTM & ICPs
**Problem:** Single generic homepage for all verticals. BFSI, healthcare, and edtech buyers land on the same page with no vertical-specific proof, compliance callouts, or demo flows.

**Fix:** Build 3 vertical landing pages (`/bfsi`, `/healthcare`, `/edtech`) with vertical-specific hero, a pre-built demo call clip, logos, compliance callout, and segment-specific CTA.

---

### #2 — UX
**Problem:** "GET STARTED" routes to a contact form — no interactive demo, no sandbox, no sample call to hear. Voice AI is an audio product; buyers can't evaluate quality without hearing it.

**Fix:** Embed a no-signup live demo call on the homepage. Add a sandbox template in the Call Flow Builder. Surface docs prominently. Ships in one sprint.

---

### #3 — Features / Services
**Problem:** Voice analytics (emotion, intent, tone detection) is buried as one of five homepage bullets. No dedicated page, no dashboard screenshot, no standalone pricing. A high-margin product line left on the table.

**Fix:** Launch "VocalInsights" — standalone analytics tier with its own landing page, dashboard mockup, per-call pricing, and a free 10-call upload trial. Upsell path to full automation.

---

### #4 — Competitor Analysis
**Problem:** No comparison page, no India-specific benchmarks. "India-first" is claimed but never proven. Bolna, VAPI, and Retell own the comparison narrative Vocallabs is absent from.

**Fix:** Publish a "Vocallabs vs the rest" page with India-specific benchmarks — language coverage (Hindi, Marathi, Tamil etc.), latency on Indian networks, INR pricing, data residency, and local telephony integrations.

---

### #5 — Potential Collaborations
**Problem:** Integrations are undiscoverable. CRM sync mentioned only in blogs. No integrations page, no telephony partner mentions (Exotel, Ozonetel). Zero partner co-marketing.

**Fix:** Build `/integrations` page (CRM, Telephony, Workflow, Analytics). Partner with Exotel + LeadSquared for co-marketing. List on their partner marketplaces.
